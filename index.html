About us

Hexa's Gobindaganj is one of the best IELTS center.

We are working in English language and computer education through a total of 14 campuses in Sylhet and Moulvibazar districts with the great objective of creating skilled human resources. We have Cambridge certified faculty and state-of the-art course materials. HEXA'S EDUCATION received top performer award by British Council in IELTS course training in Bangladesh.
